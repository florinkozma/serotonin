Serotonin is a minimal typography-based WordPress theme for personal blogs. The clean and simple layout allows your visitors to focus entirely on your awesome content.
 
== Description ==
 
Serotonin aims to simplify your WordPress experience with simple to use configurations and its typography and image based engine, clean design and focus on readability. It also has a custom search results page for your visitors to easily find what they are looking for It is very well written and tested and we offer fast technical support.


== Installation ==

1. Upload `serotonin.php` to the `/wp-content/theme/` directory
2. Activate the theme through the 'Themes' menu in WordPress
